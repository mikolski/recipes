Ingredients:
 -2 slices of brown bread or brown baguette
 -butter
 -smoked bacon
 -stilton
 -beetroot chutney
 -coriander
 -spring onion

Directions:
 1. Spread butter on one slice of bread or baguette and beetroot chutney on another
 
 2. Place bacon on the slice then stilton, sprinkle with coriander and spring onion
 
 3. Cover with another slice and enjoy fusion of flavours with this simple yet entertaining piece of sandwich
